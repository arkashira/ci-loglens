### Narrative Flow (Quick Reference)

1. **Aware** – Developer hits a noisy CI failure → sees a short demo → clicks to learn more.  
2. **Consider** – Reads detailed material, evaluates integration & security → signs up for sandbox.  
3. **Try** – Runs ci‑loglens on a real failing build, gets AI‑highlighted error clusters → impressed, decides to trial.  
4. **Adopt** – Pays for subscription, monitors reduced MTTR, integrates alerts, scales across repos.  
5. **Expand** – Purchases enterprise tier, trains custom models, brings other teams on board, drives higher ARR.
