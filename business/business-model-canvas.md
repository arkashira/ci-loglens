# Business Model Canvas

## Customer Segments
- DevOps teams at mid to large-sized companies (50-500 employees) managing complex CI/CD pipelines
- Software development teams experiencing frequent build failures and debugging challenges
- Cloud service providers offering CI/CD solutions to their customers
- Enterprise organizations with complex multi-environment deployments
- Open-source contributors and maintainers of CI/CD tools

## Value Propositions
- AI-powered parsing and visualization of CI logs to reduce debugging time by 70%
- Automated error identification and categorization to accelerate issue resolution
- Integration with popular CI/CD platforms (Jenkins, GitHub Actions, GitLab CI, CircleCI)
- Historical trend analysis to identify recurring issues and prevent future failures
- Real-time alerting system for critical failures with customizable thresholds

## Channels
- Direct online sales through Axentx's website with tiered pricing ($50-500/month)
- Integration marketplace partnerships with CI/CD platform providers
- Developer conferences and DevOps-focused events for product demonstrations
- Content marketing through technical blog posts and case studies
- Free tier with limited features to drive adoption and upsell opportunities

## Customer Relationships
- Self-service model with comprehensive documentation and knowledge base
- Premium support packages with response times within 4 hours for critical issues
- Community forums for peer-to-peer support and feature requests
- Regular webinars showcasing new features and best practices
- Dedicated account managers for enterprise customers with 50+ seats

## Revenue Streams
- Tiered SaaS subscriptions: Basic ($49/month), Pro ($149/month), Enterprise ($499/month)
- Annual prepaid discounts (20% off monthly rates)
- Integration fees for premium third-party CI/CD platform connections
- Custom development services for enterprise-specific requirements
- Data analytics add-ons for advanced trend analysis and reporting

## Key Resources
- AI/ML models for log parsing and error identification
- Development team with expertise in CI/CD systems and log analysis
- Customer support infrastructure with ticketing system
- Integration APIs for connecting with various CI/CD platforms
- Secure cloud infrastructure for data processing and storage

## Key Activities
- Continuous improvement of AI models with new log pattern recognition
- Development and maintenance of platform integrations
- Customer support and issue resolution
- Marketing and sales activities targeting DevOps teams
- Regular security audits and compliance updates

## Key Partners
- CI/CD platform providers (Jenkins, GitHub, GitLab, CircleCI)
- Cloud service providers (AWS, Azure, Google Cloud)
- DevOps consulting firms for implementation services
- Open-source communities for feedback and contributions
- Technology partners for complementary DevOps tools

- Research and development: $120,000/year (THB 4,200,000)
- Infrastructure and hosting: $60,000/year (THB 2,100,000)
- Sales and marketing: $80,000/year (THB 2,800,000)
- Customer support: $40,000/year (THB 1,400,000)
- Administrative and operational costs: $50,000/year (THB 1,750,000)
